Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to VASMU-STORE. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or make a purchase.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with VASMU-STORE, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Details: Your name, email address (e.g., when signing up for Early Drop Access), shipping address, and phone number when placing an order.
  • Payment Information: Secure payment details required to process your transaction. (Note: We use secure third-party payment gateways and do not store your credit card numbers).
  • Browsing Data: Information about your device, IP address, and how you navigate our site to help us improve the user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly for the following purposes:

  • To process, fulfill, and dispatch your orders within our 48-hour window.
  • To communicate with you regarding your order status, returns (under our 7-Day Free Return policy), and customer support inquiries.
  • To send you 24-hour early drop notifications and exclusive offers, provided you have opted into our newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • To maintain and improve our website's functionality and security.

4. Data Protection & Security

Your privacy is our priority. We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We limit access to your data only to those employees and partners who need it to fulfill your order.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share necessary data only with trusted service providers (such as shipping partners and payment processors) solely for the purpose of operating our business and delivering your products.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the personal information we hold about you. If you wish to exercise these rights or unsubscribe from our mailing list, please contact us using the information below.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at:

  • Email: support@vasmustore.com
  • Business Hours: Monday - Saturday, 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM (IST)
